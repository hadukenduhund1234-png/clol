ℹ️ How it works
Browsing & signing up
1
At the top of the home screen you see the live Upcoming Events dashboard. Below that are all Categories. Click any card to open it.
2
Inside a category you see all Lists — each one has a date, optional time, channel, and a slot progress bar.
3
Click a list card to open its detail view. Click any free slot, enter your nickname and press "Sign up ✓".
Upcoming dashboard
4
The dashboard shows all upcoming events with live countdowns and their channel. Cards turn yellow when < 1 hour remains, red & pulsing within 15 minutes.
5
Once an event's time has passed, it disappears from the dashboard automatically.
Removing your sign-up
6
Open the list detail, hover your slot and click ✕. Enter your name and a reason — the admin reviews and accepts or denies your request.
Tip: The "Participants" dashboard below the list cards shows all names at a glance.
Note: Guests cannot delete lists or categories. Contact an admin if needed.
Logging in
1
Click "Admin Login" and enter the admin password (set via ADMIN_PASSWORD env var in Railway). Session lasts 24 hours.
Managing categories & lists
2
Click "+ New Category" on the home screen. Hover cards to reveal ✏️ Edit and 🗑 Delete buttons.
3
When creating a list, set the Channel (1–7) along with date and optional time. The channel is shown everywhere — cards, dashboard, and detail view.
4
Inside a list detail, hover a taken slot and click ✕ to immediately remove the sign-up.
Removal requests
5
When a guest requests removal, a red badge appears on 🔔 Requests. Click to review and accept or deny each request.
Remember: All deletes are permanent and cannot be undone.
