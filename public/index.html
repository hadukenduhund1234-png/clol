ℹ️ How it works
Browsing & signing up
1
At the top you see the live Upcoming Events dashboard. Below are all Categories.
2
Inside a category you see all Lists — each has a date, optional time, channel and slot progress bar.
3
Click a list card. Click any free slot, enter your nickname, choose Sure ✅ or Maybe 🟡, and sign up.
4
If you choose Maybe, a second person can register as Standby 🟠 (replacement). If the maybe cancels, standby takes the spot.
💡 Tip: Events turn yellow when < 1 hour away, red & pulsing within 15 minutes. Slots with a removal request glow red.
Managing categories & lists
1
Log in → "+ New Category". Hover cards for ✏️ Edit / 🗑 Delete.
2
Use ✏️ Edit on any list card to change its title, date, slots or channel.
3
Accept or deny slot-removal requests via the 🔔 bell. Expired lists are auto-deleted after their event time passes.
4
In the Marketplace, hover any listing to reveal a delete button.
Note: All deletes are permanent and cannot be undone.
